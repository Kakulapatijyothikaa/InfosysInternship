About Our Project


Iris tumors are rare but can significantly affect vision and eye health. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and better patient outcomes. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) provide advanced techniques for image analysis in medical diagnostics.

The iris is a key feature in ocular diagnostics, providing vital information about various conditions . High-resolution imaging techniques can capture detailed features of the iris, aiding in tumor detection. Accurate imaging is essential for effective CNN training and subsequent tumor identification.

Image preprocessing involves enhancing the quality of images for better analysis by CNNS.
